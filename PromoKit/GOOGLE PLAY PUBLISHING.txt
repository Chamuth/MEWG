MEWG - Multiplayer English Word Matching Game

A Multiplayer English Word Matching game

FULL DESCRIPTION [4000]

A fun and engaging game to improve your vocabulary by challenging other players all around the world 🌎

With lightning fast real-time multiplayer technologies, you can join and play a round of word matching instantly ⚡

The fastest player to match all or most words in a hidden crossword puzzle within 5 minutes, wins the game, creating an exciting and intense battle between two players 🏁

<b>⭐ FEATURES ⭐</b>
⚪ SIMPLISTIC: We value your time and won't try to waste it; Just press the play button and start matching with a random player ⌚
⚪ CHALLENGE ANYONE: A fast matchmaking server to find someone to play multiplayer word matching with, even if you don't have any friends 👨‍👦‍👦
⚪ LIVE REACTIONS: React to your opponent's playing to make the match more interesting and fun 🎉
⚪ HINTS SYSTEM: Use hints to help you jog your memory of vocabulary to easily match the words and win. Get free hints daily by playing on subsequent days 😉
⚪ LEVEL UP: Gain XP points and level up to get yourself titles and medals 🏅
⚪ CREATED FOR ALL AGES: This word matching game is created for all ages; kids and adults both can play without having to worry about anything 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
⚪ REDUCE YOUR STRESS: A friendly competition to reduce your stress and keep the blood flowing 😊
⚪ LOGIN WITH SOCIAL MEDIA: Just tap on your preferred social media button and start playing with opponents from all around the globe 📶
⚪ CHALLENGES: A lot of challenges and achivements to finish, even by just participating on the game for subsequent days 🏆
⚪ 100% FREE FOR ALL: The entire word matching experience is free for all. No need to pay for hidden content in the game. Any level or word set is completely free 🆓
⚪ LEADERBOARD: A real time leaderboard to find out who's the best in the entire world, measured from Win/Loss Ratio and Experience Points (XP), so the more you play the more you'll get your name up the list 📃

<b>⭐ INSTRUCTIONS TO PLAY ⭐</b>
⚪ START A MATCH: Just press the play button on the main menu to match with another player randomly selected from the entire world. ▶
⚪ MATCH WORDS: Drag through letters to form up a word that is suitable for the hidden blocks you see on the crossword. 👆
⚪ FIND 'EM ALL AND WIN: Match and find words as much as you can to beat the other player and gain more XP with the added vocabulary training. 🏆😜

We hope you enjoy MEWG and leave every word matching round satisfied with the challenge it brings. Download MEWG now to challenge everyone and anyone. Good Luck! 😘
